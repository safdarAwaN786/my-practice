What can I do

UI/UX Design

The goal of a UI designer is to make the interaction with technology aesthetic, pleasurable, and useful at the same time. From the design and color of buttons to the hover effects on various links and from the transition between webpages to the use of visual elements, all decisions fall under the purview of a UI designer.

Graphic Design

A graphic designer is a professional within the graphic design and graphic arts industry who assembles together images, typography, or motion graphics to create a piece of design. A graphic designer creates the graphics primarily for published, printed, or electronic media, such as brochures and advertising.
